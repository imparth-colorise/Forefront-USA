Ander Smith is an experienced business attorney who works with clients on business-related matters, whether M&A, contracts or commercial real estate.

Included among the services Ander provides is working with clients on preparing for and assisting in the sale of a business. He first takes the time to explain how the process tends to unfold; this understanding can greatly mitigate the anxiousness that’s normal for a business owner contemplating the sale of their business. Once a client has a good understanding of what to expect, and Ander has a good understanding of the business, he then works with his client to undertake a pre-sale assessment of the business well before starting any third-party conversations, and only once the internal "house" is in order, they brainstorm appropriate potential buyer parties. He then guides the process of offer inquiries, through the negotiating purchase, all the way to closing.
